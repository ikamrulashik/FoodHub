Online shoppers experience a lot of friction and that friction prevents the customer from buying from your site, as well as decreases your Shopify conversion rate. The biggest friction is the fear of being deceived, such as paying money but not receiving anything, or the thing you receive is not the same as what you see on the internet. That said, brand trust is one of the most important factors that affect eCommerce sales and Shopify conversion rates. The first and most important thing to build brand trust is to tell the customer who you really are, with unique and trustful identity. That is the reason why Shopify about us pages become one of the most important elements that help increase Shopify conversion rate.
